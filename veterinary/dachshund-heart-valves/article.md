# Dachshund Heart Valve Cycle

Hosted visuals:

- `/veterinary/dachshund-heart-valves/cycle/`
- `/veterinary/dachshund-heart-valves/maximal/`

## Short Legend

- Green: valve or pathway currently open
- Neutral gray: valve currently closed
- Particles: subtle flow direction cue rather than oversized arrows

## Key Takeaways

- AV valves open during filling and close when ventricular pressure rises above atrial pressure.
- Semilunar valves open only after ventricular pressure exceeds the great arteries.
- Valve closure sounds reflect pressure reversal, not an independent motor action by the valves.
