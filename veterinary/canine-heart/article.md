# How a Canine Heart Works

Hosted visual:

- `/veterinary/canine-heart/how-it-works/`

## Short Legend

- Blue: blood headed toward the lungs
- Red: blood headed toward the body
- Green highlights: open valves or active forward flow

## Key Takeaways

- The right heart sends blood to the lungs and the left heart sends blood to the body.
- Valves open only when pressure favors forward flow.
- The electrical trigger coordinates timing, but the pressure gradient determines valve motion.
